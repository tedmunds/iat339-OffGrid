About Solar Panels

Solar panels are the power source for your off grid system. When exposed to sunlight, the generate a constant stream of clean energy, which can be stored in your batteries.

An inverter transforms the raw DC electricity from the panels into AC current that appliances and devices in your house use. You can also use a monitor to track your power useage on an hour by hour basis.
