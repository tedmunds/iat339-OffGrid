About us

At Off Grid it is our goal to help regular people build their own complete off grid power system. That is not an easy task, so we provide you with info about every step of the journey ahead.

The basis of most power systems is the power source, like an array of solar arrays or a big ole pipe in the ground that captures the raw heat from deep in the earth. This power source generates electricity in it's raw form known as DC current, or direct current. This electricity needs to be stored somewhere so that you can use it later, which is where batteries come in.

Now the problem is that when you want to use the electricty to charge your phone, it needs to be turned into Alternating current, which is where an Inverter comes in. This magical piece of hardware transforms the power and distributes it to your home.

It doesn't stop there though, the sky is really the limit. D you want to automate you solar panels to rack the sun across the sky? You can do that. Do you want to track every minute of power you generate? You can do that too. How about sell power back to your local provider? Well, thats not on us but maybe!
