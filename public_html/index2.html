Welcome to OffGrid!

There are several different types of batteries, including the standard Lead-Acid type. They use chemical processes to store electricity for long periods of time. They are vital to your off grid system so that you can generate electricity, and then use it when you need it, like at night.

Things to consider when looking at batteries are things like; how long will you need to store power? Does your power generation system only generate power during part of the day? For solar, you will need enough battery storage to last you a few days in case it gets cloudy for a while!

An inverter transforms the raw DC electricity from the panels into AC current that appliances and devices in your house use. It also manages your batteries so that they only output your electricity when you really need it.
